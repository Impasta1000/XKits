What is XKits?

XKits is basically a KitPVP plugin. It is designed for servers which encompass the PVP Game Mode, adding a twist to the game and adding kits with special abilities (in future implementations)

XKits also offers its own kit creation feature, where players/server admins can create their own kits to use.
